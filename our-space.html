Enhancing the performance and Productivity of farms by real-time monitoring of farms

We are a farm project-management company established to provide both cloud-based Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) to enable real-time monitoring for private and cluster farms for improved farm operations

Who We Serve

This service is suitable for individuals and entities looking to gain proper visibility
into farm projects and assess farm performance.

Potential users of this service include:

  • National & Sub-National Governments - Federal & State Ministries of Agriculture & Rural Development
  • Financial institutions-DMBs, MFBs, DFls such as AFDB, CBN, DBN & NIRSAL
  • Investors (both private & institutional)
  • Local and Foreign Aggregators and off-takers
  • Agricultural Insurers
  • NGOs & INGOs- USAID, DFID, WFP, IFAD, etc.
  • Commercial Farms
  • Smallholder farmers

Why We are Different

Think of us as a 'thermostat' and our competitors as 'thermometers'. We go beyond telling client things are going right or wrong to ensuring things go right on our projects. All the data and elements tracked by our system are analyzed by Artificial Intelligence and integrated to ensure progressive improvement in yield per hectare of crop and profitability.

  • We can easily scale across multiple crop and multiple location (Nationally and Across Africa)
  • We prioritize ‘lessons learnt’ across farms across location to enhance continuous improvement
  • Proven experience in service deployment
  • We are indigenous, we understand the dynamics and 'language' of or local farmers
  • Our real-time data is both satellite and onsite driven
  • Unique and user friendly FarmMonitorTM tool
